    • Ramen

    Descriprion

    One of my favorite things to eat, could be lunch, late dinner, or even a midnight snack. So easy and simple to make, full of flavor and spices, tastes so good.

  • Water
  • microwave
  • bowl

    • Steps

    1. Fill bowl of ramen with water up to right above the pieces of noodles
    2. Pour the spice or flavor packet in to the bowl of ramen
    3. put into the microwave for 3 minutes
    4. Once done cooking, take out and let it cool for a bit, then enjoy :)

    Egg sandwhich

    Description

    Also showed to me by my parents, when i first ate it, i loved it. Since then i have been shown how to make it and now i use as a lunch or a late dinner, filled with protien and calories, it tastes great.

  • Toasted bread
  • 2-3 eggs
  • pan and spatula
    1. spray the pan over the stove with PAM or sometime of substance to have non stickyness
    2. Toast the bread between light and meduim
    3. cook the eggs slowly at a medium heat so it can stay together as 1-2 pieces
    4. Slide the egg to the toast and close, cut and eat
